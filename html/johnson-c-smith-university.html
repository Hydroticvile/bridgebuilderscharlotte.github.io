Crisis and Compassion

An Interfaith Documentary Series

When the pandemic hit in March, 2020, the interfaith organization MeckMIN stepped up efforts to connect those suffering under its fallout with faith communities and non-profits well-positioned to respond. The JCSU team, in turn, stepped up to capture and tell this story in a series of documentaries called "Crisis and Compassion." (Pictured right: LeDayne McLeese Polaski, MeckMIN's Executive Director)
