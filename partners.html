Our Campus and Community Partners

Crossing campus. Crossing town. Crossing worldview.

Crossing campus. Crossing town. Crossing worldview.

Bridge Builders Charlotte is all about partnership. In our initial phase, The Gambrell Foundation provided startup funding as Queens University's Belk Chapel joined forces with Chicago-based Interfaith Youth Core (IFYC) to build a network of campus-community partnerships. Along the way, we're grateful for the wisdom and support from community leaders serving on our Strategic Leadership Council. Now, The Duke Endowment has joined our team, providing an expansion grant that allows us to broaden and deepen vaccine outreach among vulnerable groups in the Carolinas.
