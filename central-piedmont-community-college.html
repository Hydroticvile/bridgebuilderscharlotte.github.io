Central Piedmont Community College

An Interfaith Response to Food Insecurity

A team from Central Piedmont Community College (CPCC) partnered with Loaves and Fishes to address food insecurity, which has skyrocketed during the pandemic. CPCC interns expanded food drives to include provisions sourced according to Muslim and Hindu traditions. Along the way, they've connected with diverse faith community leaders to learn more about specific needs and how they can be met.
