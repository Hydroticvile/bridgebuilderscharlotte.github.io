Queens University

Building Interfaith Capacity in a Secular Non-Profit

When COVID-19 hit Charlotte, vulnerable families who depend on the public schools to feed their families found themselves in crisis. OurBRIDGE for KIDS stepped up, shifting from it's emphasis on after-school tutoring for immigrant and refugee children to meet their families' basic need for food. Queen's team reached out to this community partner, providing training, a promotional brochure, and a Ramadan celebration, all designed to engage the religiously diverse community to which ourBRIDGE belongs.
